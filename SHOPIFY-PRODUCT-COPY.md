<h2>The Only CRM Built for Aircraft Detailers</h2>

<p>
  You got into detailing because you love making aircraft shine — not because you love spreadsheets, back-of-napkin math, and chasing down payments. Shiny Jets CRM replaces the guesswork with a purpose-built platform that handles quoting, scheduling, payments, and customer management so you can focus on the work.
</p>

<p>
  Built by a professional aircraft detailer with 15+ years in the industry, every feature exists because it was needed in a real detailing business.
</p>

<hr>

<h3>Smart Quote Builder — 208 Aircraft Pre-Loaded</h3>
<p>
  Select from 208 aircraft models with pre-loaded dimensions, surface areas, and default service hours. Set your hourly rate, pick the aircraft, choose your services — Shiny Jets CRM calculates the price instantly. Build a professional quote in under 60 seconds instead of 30 minutes. Supports custom services, packages, bundle discounts, and add-on fees.
</p>

<h3>Customer Portal — Online Acceptance and Payment</h3>
<p>
  Send branded quote links to your clients via email or text. Customers view a professional quote page with your logo, colors, and branding — then accept and pay online with one click. Deposits, full payments, and credit card fee pass-through are all built in. Connect your Stripe account and start getting paid faster.
</p>

<h3>Job Scheduling — Calendar and Team Management</h3>
<p>
  Accepted quotes automatically create jobs on your calendar. Assign team members, track hours, manage contractors, and get reminders before every appointment. Your crew can log time with a simple PIN code — no app download required. See your week at a glance and never double-book again.
</p>

<h3>Analytics Dashboard — Know Your Numbers</h3>
<p>
  Track revenue, quote conversion rates, average job value, platform fees, and monthly trends. See which services generate the most revenue and which aircraft types you detail most often. Monthly ROI reports delivered to your inbox show exactly how much time and money the platform is saving your business.
</p>

<h3>White-Label Branding — Your Logo, Colors, Fonts</h3>
<p>
  Upload your logo, set your brand colors, choose heading and body fonts. Every quote, email, invoice, and customer portal page reflects your brand — not ours. On Pro plans and above, remove the Shiny Jets badge entirely. Your customers see your company, not ours.
</p>

<hr>

<h3>What Detailers Are Saying</h3>
<blockquote>
  <p>"Shiny Jets CRM saves me 10 hours a week on quoting alone. I used to spend 30 minutes per quote — now it takes 60 seconds."</p>
  <cite>— Professional Aircraft Detailer, Scottsdale, AZ</cite>
</blockquote>

<hr>

<h3>Frequently Asked Questions</h3>

<details>
  <summary><strong>What is Shiny Jets CRM?</strong></summary>
  <p>Shiny Jets CRM is a quoting, scheduling, and customer management platform built exclusively for aircraft detailing businesses. It includes a database of 208 aircraft models, a smart quote builder, online payments via Stripe, job scheduling with team management, analytics, and white-label branding. It is used by professional aircraft detailers across the United States and internationally.</p>
</details>

<details>
  <summary><strong>How does the quote builder work?</strong></summary>
  <p>Select an aircraft model from the built-in database of 208 types — from a Cessna 172 to a Gulfstream G700. Each aircraft has pre-loaded dimensions and default service hours. Choose your services (wash, polish, ceramic coating, interior detail, etc.), and the system calculates the price based on your hourly rate multiplied by the aircraft-specific hours. You can adjust hours, add custom services, apply discounts, and send the quote in under 60 seconds.</p>
</details>

<details>
  <summary><strong>Can my customers pay online?</strong></summary>
  <p>Yes. When you send a quote, your customer receives a branded link where they can review the services, accept the quote, and pay online via credit card. Shiny Jets CRM supports full payments, deposit payments, and optional credit card fee pass-through. Payments are processed through your own Stripe account — funds go directly to your bank.</p>
</details>

<details>
  <summary><strong>Is there a free plan?</strong></summary>
  <p>Yes. The Free plan includes up to 3 quotes per month, the full 208 aircraft database, email quote sharing, and Stripe payments. There is a 5% platform fee on completed jobs. Paid plans (Pro at $79/mo, Business at $149/mo, Enterprise at $299/mo) offer unlimited quotes, lower platform fees, team management, custom branding, and priority support.</p>
</details>

<details>
  <summary><strong>Do I need to install anything?</strong></summary>
  <p>No. Shiny Jets CRM is a web application that runs in your browser on any device — desktop, tablet, or phone. There is nothing to download or install. It also works as a Progressive Web App (PWA), so you can add it to your home screen for a native app experience.</p>
</details>

<hr>

<p style="text-align: center; margin-top: 2em;">
  <a href="https://crm.shinyjets.com/signup" style="display: inline-block; background-color: #007CB1; color: #ffffff; padding: 16px 40px; text-decoration: none; font-weight: 600; font-size: 16px; border-radius: 4px;">
    Start Free at crm.shinyjets.com
  </a>
</p>

<p style="text-align: center; color: #888; font-size: 13px;">
  No credit card required. Upgrade anytime.
</p>
