<h2>Stop Wasting Hours on Aircraft Detailing Quotes</h2>

<p>
  If you're still pricing aircraft detailing jobs with spreadsheets, calculators, or gut instinct — you're leaving money on the table and burning hours you could spend detailing. The average detailer spends 20-30 minutes building each quote manually. With Shiny Jets CRM Pro, that drops to 60 seconds.
</p>

<p>

</p>

<hr>

<h3>Smart Quote Builder — 60 Seconds, Not 30 Minutes</h3>
<p>
  Select from 208 pre-loaded aircraft models — from a Cessna 172 to a Boeing 787. Each model includes factory dimensions, estimated surface area, and default service hours. Set your hourly rate, choose your services, and the quote builds itself. Add custom services, packages, bundle discounts, and add-on fees. Send via email or shareable link.
</p>

<h3>208 Aircraft Database — Every Make and Model</h3>
<p>
  Pre-loaded with 208 aircraft types spanning single-engine piston, turboprop, light jet, midsize jet, heavy jet, ultra-long-range, wide-body, helicopter, and military/warbird categories. Each entry includes default interior and exterior service hours based on real detailing data from working professionals.
</p>

<h3>Customer Portal — Professional Online Acceptance</h3>
<p>
  Your customers receive a branded quote page with your logo, colors, and fonts. They can review line items, accept with one click, and pay online immediately. No more chasing signatures or waiting for wire transfers. Supports deposits, full payments, and credit card fee pass-through.
</p>

<h3>Online Payments via Stripe</h3>
<p>
  Connect your own Stripe account and accept credit card payments directly through your quotes. Funds deposit to your bank account. Platform handles payment processing, receipts, and refunds. You stay in control — Shiny Jets CRM never holds your money.
</p>

<h3>Analytics Dashboard — Know Your Numbers</h3>
<p>
  Track monthly revenue, quote-to-job conversion rates, average job value, busiest months, and platform fee savings. See which aircraft types and services drive the most revenue. Monthly ROI reports show exactly how much time and money the platform saves your business.
</p>

<h3>Automated Follow-Ups — Never Lose a Lead</h3>
<p>
  Shiny Jets CRM tracks when customers open your quotes. If they don't open it in 2 days, you get notified. If they view but don't book in 3 days, you get a reminder. Five days before expiry, the customer gets an automatic reminder. Two days before, an optional discount offer closes the deal.
</p>

<p>

<details>
  <summary><strong>How is the price calculated on each quote?</strong></summary>
  <p>Shiny Jets CRM multiplies your hourly rate by the service hours for each aircraft model. For example, if you charge $190/hour and a Gulfstream G450 exterior wash takes 3.5 hours, the quote auto-calculates to $665. You can adjust hours, add services, apply discounts, or override the price at any time.</p>
</details>

</details>
